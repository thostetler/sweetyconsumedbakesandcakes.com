Why I Love What I Do

It's true, even with the simplest of things we can make them beautiful with a little creation and imagination. I love to bake everything from fresh baked goods, pies, cakes, cheesecakes, cookies and so much more. For me though it's not just "baking". I love that you can take a simple cake/dessert and turn it into a piece of art! It's such a reward for me to see the reactions and friendships that bring people together over food. It is the one common ground we all share.

Everything from bridal showers, baby showers, weddings, birthdays, anniversary's, picnics, holidays or even just a day that is a "just because" day... How often do you know somebody that needs a little pick me up or a little something to just say "I'm thinking of you".

Someday's I think there isn't anything that a piece of cake can't solve....then I look at my yard, cake obliviously won't replace mowing the yard...unless I could bribe someone to mow the yard for a piece of cake ;) ) haha. Sorry, just a little humor.

While I would love to say that I am an expert who has mastered it all, I would be lying to you and myself... The truth is, I hope that no matter how old I get or the amount of experience that I will have, I hope that I will always be learning new techniques and new things! The cake industry is always changing and is one that allows your imagination to run wild with creation! My wish is to always keep learning and to always be challenged by the new, whatever that may be. I am one that will strive for perfection until I do have it perfected, but I am always on the search for a challenge.

Thank you so much, and I look forward to hearing from you and hopefully being able to be a part of your truly special memory.
